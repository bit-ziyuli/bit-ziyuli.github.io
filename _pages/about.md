I am an Assistant Researcher at the School of Computer Science, **Beijing Institute of Technology (BIT)**, affiliated with the Brain-Computer Interface and Brain-Inspired Intelligence Research Center, supervised by Prof. Xia Wu. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Application Technology from Beijing Normal University in 2024.

My research focuses on **Brain Science and Brain-Inspired Intelligence (脑科学与类脑智能)**, including neural decoding, brain-computer interfaces, and brain-inspired AI architectures. I have published **27 papers**, with **13 as first/corresponding author**, including 5 CAS Zone 1 Top journals, 2 CCF-A conferences (NeurIPS, CVPR), and 3 CCF-B conferences. I have been granted **4 invention patents** and received total research funding of over **1.23 million RMB**.

I serve as a reviewer for prestigious journals including *Pattern Recognition*, *Neural Networks*, and *Neurocomputing*, as well as top conferences such as NeurIPS, AAAI, ICLR, ICML, and MICCAI.
